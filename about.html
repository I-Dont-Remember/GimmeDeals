Why Make Madtown Deals?

Because we have heard about and promptly forgot about tons of great deals we wanted to visit. Now there is one quick spot for anyone to check.

There are so many great food deals happening in Madison, but apps like Hooked only cover the short term ones. We wanted a place we could check for regular great deals. People should be able to be hungry on a given day, quick check our site and know all the awesome deals they could snag that very same night.

This is a crowd-sourced tool, so for it to be useful people have to submit their ideas, especially if you think the business is run by great people and deserves the press. Please enjoy it, and if there are any comments/concerns/questions/ideas for improving the site please use the email below. We hope this saves you money and finds you a good time!
